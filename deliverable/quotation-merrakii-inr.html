1. Executive summary

This quotation covers requirement gathering, design, engineering, quality assurance, deployment support, and project management for Merrakii—a student-centred web platform for guided discovery, institute & services selection, structured application, payment, and post-payment confirmation.

Commercial delivery is organised in three phases. Phase 1 is MVP development of the web application (the programme and fees in the tables that follow). Phase 2 is native mobile application development (iOS and Android), priced and scheduled separately when you choose to proceed. Phase 3 is an annual retain-and-maintain (AMC) contract for ongoing production support after go-live.

When development (Phase 1) is complete, you receive three months of complimentary maintenance on the delivered scope. After that period, you may opt into Phase 3 under a separate AMC, or continue with change-controlled enhancements as agreed.

Tarun Rastogi leads this engagement for the client. He brings strong delivery ownership, strategic and leadership judgement, project management discipline, and hands-on development experience, with 15 years in industry across multinational companies, building high-quality applications deployed for demanding, global-class clients.

3. Inclusions

  • Discovery workshops for the signed scope (within agreed limits) and a written scope baseline.
  • User experience and interface design for surfaces agreed in the statement of work.
  • Engineering, automated and manual testing, and deployment support for agreed environments.
  • Project management embedded in the agreed professional fees.
  • Application hosting for agreed development, staging, and production environments during the delivery programme, as defined in the statement of work.
  • Third-party API integration charges used in explore, discovery, application, post-payment, user dashboard, and vendor dashboard.

4. Exclusions

  • Client-owned content, catalog accuracy, institute contracts, and legal or policy copy.
  • Third-party spend outside what Inclusions expressly cover — for example: payment gateway charges; Android hosting charges; Google Play Store hosting or distribution charges; Apple App Store hosting or distribution charges; and other tools or API integrations not within the application hosting and third-party API integration inclusion items above, or not within the phases defined elsewhere in this quotation.
  • Formal penetration testing by a named firm, formal legal opinions, and formal WCAG certification unless separately scoped.
  • Ongoing production support after the agreed warranty window (available under a separate retainer).
