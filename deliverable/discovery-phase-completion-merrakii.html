Phase 1 — Discovery: Completion Summary

Formal record that Discovery for the Merrakii website initiative is complete and the agreed artefacts are accepted for handover into the next project phase(s).

Purpose of the Discovery Phase

Discovery aligns the vendor and client on measurable outcomes, audience needs, scope boundaries, technical constraints, and how success will be validated before substantial design or build investment. Outputs from this phase de-risk downstream work by freezing a credible baseline requirement set and roadmap.
