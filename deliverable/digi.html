MERRAKII · MARKET REALITY

THE WHY

Students struggle to convert intent into enrollment

Structural inefficiencies persist across the admissions journey, creating friction for students and limiting institutional growth.

  • 1 ·Choice overload

    Students face too many options, delaying confident academic decisions.

  • 2 ·Institute discovery gap

    Institution comparison remains fragmented due to inconsistent information.

  • 3 ·Fragmented discovery

    Research is scattered across portals, PDFs, and chat threads—with no single surface to weigh options side by side.

  • 4 ·Enrollment friction

    Manual workflows cause delays and student drop-offs before admission.

Merrakii · Product promise

The "what"

From interest to enrollment — in one AI-powered surface

A single place to move from intent to a confirmed seat — with guidance at every step.

  • 1

    One-stop journey

    Students go from their stream of interest to enrolling in the right program — without juggling disconnected sites and forms.

  • 2

    AI-powered courses, enrollment & guidance

    Discovery, structured intake, and in-app assistance work together so exploration doesn’t end at a dead PDF — users get answers and next steps when it matters.

  • 3

    No more missed enrollments

    Deadlines, eligibility, and seat risk surfaced early — fewer drop-offs from lack of awareness or forgotten follow-ups.

  • 4

    Personalized dashboards — students & vendors

    Students track applications, courses, progress, and next steps in one place. Vendors run listings, enrollments, cohorts, and performance through role-based views.

  • 5

    Value-added services with each enrollment

    Mock tests, evaluations, study material, live and recorded sessions, doubt clearing, assignments with feedback, progress analytics, and certificates — packaged per course and phased by institute.

Our bet is straightforward: when intelligence sits inside discovery, documents, guidance, and day-to-day operations—not in a sidecar chat—students advance faster and institutes keep control.

Merrakii · Product story

The "how"

Intelligence woven through the journey

One platform where AI builds trust and measurable progress to enrollment.

  • 1

    Intelligent discovery

    Semantic search, program matching, and explainable recommendations across fields, exams, institutes, and programs.

  • 2

    Document intelligence

    Capture, classify, and validate application materials — fewer round trips, cleaner submissions.

  • 3

    Guided assistance

    Embedded copilots for students and counselors — one prompt to progress, with guardrails and citations.

  • 4

    Predictive operations

    Forecasts bottlenecks, prioritizes queues, and surfaces next best action for speed and compliance.

Outcome: A calmer, faster path for learners; a defensible, audit-friendly operating rhythm for partners—without sacrificing the human relationship.

Merrakii · Horizon

Timeline & support

Cadence & stewardship

Milestone durations · Post–go-live stewardship

12 weeks end-to-end when run sequentially (4 + 2 + 2 + 2 + 2): executive discovery, architecture & design sign-off, a time-boxed web engineering sprint, validation & release readiness, then commercial go-live & handover. Timelines assume your inputs and third-party readiness; after go-live, bounded stewardship covers defects, patches, and platform health per agreement—excluding net-new scope unless separately scoped.

Merrakii · Commercial

Milestone model

Milestone billing & what sits outside it

Per-milestone development fees · Post–12-week stewardship & later phases priced separately

Each bill lines up with one delivery milestone on the cadence slide and covers development for that milestone only. Indicative INR fees for the baseline programme appear in the price quotation; binding amounts are in the signed proposal.

  1. 01

    M1 · Executive discovery & charter · 4w · ₹1L

    Dev fee invoice on acceptance of discovery deliverables and scoped outline.

  2. 02

    M2 · Architecture & design sign-off · 2w · ₹2L

    Dev fee invoice on acceptance of architecture / design baseline for build.

  3. 03

    M3 · Engineering sprint (web) · 2w · ₹3L

    Dev fee invoice at sprint completion & agreed test-entry / handoff criteria.

  4. 04

    M4 · Validation & readiness · 2w · ₹1L

    Dev fee invoice on testing sign-off / release readiness per agreement.

  5. 05

    M5 · Go-live & handover · 2w · ₹1L

    Dev fee invoice at production deployment / go-live per agreement.

After 12 weeks

Bounded stewardship (defects, patches, platform health — cadence slide) is its own fee, not folded into milestone dev invoices.

Later roadmap

Net-new work or follow-on releases: scoped in writing and billed separately as additional milestones or phases.

Third-party pass-through (domains, hosting, TLS, gateway) is spelled out in the signed proposal—outside milestone dev fees unless explicitly bundled.
